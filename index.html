What We Do

Residential Electrical, Done Properly

From solar to smart homes and everything in between — one trusted local team for all your electrical needs.

Solar System Installation

From 6.6kW residential systems to tailored setups for larger homes, we handle the full installation start to finish. CEC-accredited workmanship, neat cabling, and a system that's actually sized to suit how your household uses power.

Smart Home Integration

Google Nest, smart smoke and CO alarms, automated lighting and connected security — we install the tech that makes day-to-day life easier and safer. We'll walk you through how it all works before we leave.

General Residential Electrical

Powerpoints, switchboard upgrades, hob and oven changeovers, lighting, and everything in between. Tidy work, no mess left behind, and fully compliant with Australian Standards.

Security & Alarm Systems

Camera systems, alarms and hard-wired sensors installed and configured for Ballarat homes. We'll talk through realistic coverage so you're not paying for kit you don't need.

Pool & Outdoor Wiring

Safe, code-compliant wiring for pool pumps, outdoor lighting, sheds and garden power. We know the rules around wet areas inside out, so your setup is safe for every season.

Need Something Else?
